Top three activities to do in Mandalay

Visit Mandalay Palace

Explore the grand architecture and history of the Mandalay Royal Palace.

Walk on U Bein Bridge

Take a stroll on the world's oldest and longest teakwood bridge for breathtaking views.

Climb Mandalay Hill

Enjoy panoramic views of the city from the top of Mandalay Hill.

Visit Kuthodaw Pagoda

Explore the world's largest book, 729 marble slabs inscribed with the Buddhist scriptures.
